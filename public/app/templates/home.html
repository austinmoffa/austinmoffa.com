I grew up in central Pennsylvania surrounded by beautiful woodlands, scenic mountain vistas, and the sort of disturbingly regressive sociocultural attitudes that you can only find in towns without wikipedia pages.

In 2013 I moved to Washington D.C. with my fiance, as despite being in my early 20s and having a computer science degree, no one had offered millions of dollars to let me make some clever start-up with vague promises of utility and profitability.

I now work full-time as a programmer and dabble in art, writing, and music.
